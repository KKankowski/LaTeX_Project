North Kiteboarding Gambler


-HARD FLEX
-STEP DOUBLE CONCAVE SLICK BASE
-BIAX FIBRE CONSTRUCTION
-NORTH BOOT RECOMMENDED
-HUGE ROCKER
-DEEP CHANNELS
-VERY ROBUST
-STIFF FLEX FOR GOOD POP

If your passion is pure Wakestyle and Park Riding, then the Gambler is for you. From slicks to sliders, kickers and lagoons, if the kite's low you'll be riding like the Pros. Combined with the North Boot, the Gambler is a powerful performer adapting outstandingly to the variety of demands when riding without fins; after all, we want you to be able to hit the kicker for a Backside 180 then load and pop straight into a Low Mobe. North Kiteboarding Wam


-CLASSIC CONSTRUCTION
-RTM FINS
-BEST ALLROUNDER
-HIGH PERFORMANCE
-EASY TO SURF
-DRIVING BOTTOM TURNS AND FLOWING TOP TURNS

Classic, smooth and controlled, the Wam is the best allround performer no matter the conditions. It is the solution without any regrets for those who are looking for the one-for-all board in their quiver. The perfect match if you´re wanting a traditional yet stylish ride in classic surfboard shape: no wonder the Wam has gleaming reviews in magazines all over the globe: Its unique squash tail, wider nose, rock solid platform and increased rail line create a remarkable performer with outstanding light wind planing abilities. The wide tail is still loose enough for smaller waves, the straight outline ensures good control and smooth carving whenever needed.
